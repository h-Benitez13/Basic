  • You Got This!

    Second Group project: Full-stack application! You Got This! uses Node.js, MySql (as the main database), and Npm package Sequelize to create,update,delete a user's personal data. The application itself is a stress-induced-event manager where you can use to schedule and check on the upcoming events e.g. Networking, Interview or Presentation; and prepare before attending. Once creating an event, send you to your checklist page for before-event things-to-do while keeping track of total items checked off.At the end, you take a self-reflective survey, displaying a percentage of how confident you feel you did in said event!
